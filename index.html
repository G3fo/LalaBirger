ABOUT ME

DESIGNER & DEVELOPER

Hi! I'm Lala. I'm an architect, graphic designer, and a visual artist. I'm interested in art expressions, especially video games and tattoos. I'm currently studying a master of art curatorship, I've always been intrigued by art theory, and I wanted to deepen my studies. A dream of mine is to be able to break the limit between academic and non academic arts and I'll work as hard as I can towards that goal. I'm very passionate and ambitious in all my projects.

I'm a strong team worker, I've had the chance to work with a group of more than 50 people at interdisciplinary tasks, urbanizing informal and poor neighborhoods. This gave me the possibility to learn a lot from my coworkers. I value kindness and trust in my environment, I care for people and that's why I try to do the best job I can at every opportunity.
