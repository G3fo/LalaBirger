ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Lala. I'm 26 years old and I'm an architect, graphic designer, and a visual artist. I've graduated from the University of Buenos Aires in 2017 with an architectural degree and Ive been working on several art proyects in the past few years. I'm interested in art expressions, especially video games and tattoos. I'm currently studying a master of art curatorship, I've always been intrigued by art theory but I've always felt that the academic field draws a line between what can be consider art and what not. In my work I try to bend that limit by creating an artistic experience in my work eventhough it´s a small project. I'm very passionate and ambitious person.

I'm a strong team worker, I've had the chance to work with a group of more than 50 people at interdisciplinary tasks, urbanizing informal and poor neighborhoods. This gave me the possibility to learn a lot from my coworkers. I value kindness and trust in my environment, I care for people and that's why I try to do the best job I can at every opportunity.

WHAT I'M INTERESTED IN

Tattos and Videogames are both my biggest passions. I´m currently tattooing in a studio based in Buenos Aires, along lot of great artists. Nowadays, I´m working on large scale proyects, trying to reach collaborative designs by including the perspective of my client in every step of the creative process. Body movement it´s very important to me, so my work surround the idea of distortion and motion.

When I say I'm interested in videogames, I mean I'm driven by the idea of creating worlds. I think videogames are the ultimate art expression. I´ve been working for some years now, creating assets and I join for the first time last year the Global Game Jam were I had the chance to create a videogame from scratch. Luckly I was part of a team with 4 programers wich lead my to be responsible for the art. It was an amazing experience. I think my prolific and interdisciplinary path in design and art studies makes me perfect for a job in videogames. I´m especially interested in art direction
